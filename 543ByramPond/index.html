TerraNox Docks

  • ⚡ Standard 15-amp GFCI outdoor outlets
  • ⚡ Docks distributed across mow zones
  • ⚡ Multiple docks can share circuits (~200-300W each)
  • ⚡ IPX6 all-weather rated
  • ⚡ Flat, level ground with 1m clearance

FJ Dynamics RM21 Dock

  • ⚡ Standard 15-amp GFCI outdoor outlet
  • ⚡ Central location in largest flat zone
  • ⚡ ~300-500W draw
  • ⚡ Level ground with clearance for larger unit
  • ⚡ Option C only

Yarbo Docks

  • ⚡ Standard 15-amp GFCI outdoor outlets
  • ⚡ Near walkway areas for snow clearing access
  • ⚡ Can share circuits with other docks
  • ⚡ IPX5 weather-rated
  • ⚡ ~300-500W draw per unit
For Shea — Electrician Checklist:
  1. Identify panel capacity — need ~15–30A additional depending on option
  2. Option A: 11 outlets  |  Option B: 11 outlets  |  Option C: 6 outlets
  3. All standard 15A/110V — no dedicated circuits needed
  4. All outdoor outlets need weatherproof covers (in-use type)
  5. Consider conduit runs for any circuits crossing unpaved ground
  6. Option C has lowest electrical footprint — only 6 outlets needed
