Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a service plan if I buy a mower?

Service plans are optional but highly recommended. Robotic mowers need blade changes (once per season or as needed), software updates, and seasonal prep to perform their best. A service plan covers all of that and saves you money compared to individual service calls.

Can I add a service plan later?

Yes! You can add a service plan at any time. However, we recommend signing up at installation to get the best value and ensure your mower stays covered from day one.

What's included in a typical installation?

Every installation includes an on-site property survey, charging station setup, boundary mapping (wire or RTK depending on model), mower programming and configuration, zone scheduling, and a full owner training walkthrough. You'll be mowing the same day.

Do you service mowers bought elsewhere?

We service Navimow and Yarbo mowers regardless of where they were purchased. Contact us for a diagnostic assessment and we'll get your mower running right.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Westchester and Putnam County and surrounding areas within 30 miles of our Peekskill showroom. This includes Cortlandt, Yorktown, Croton, Ossining, Bedford, Katonah, Mahopac, Carmel, and more.

How long does installation take?

Most installations take 2-4 hours depending on property size and complexity. Your mower will be cutting grass the same day. We'll also spend time training you on the app and controls.

How does Mowing as a Service work?

We provide, install, and maintain a robotic mower on your property — you pay a flat monthly fee with no equipment to buy. The minimum commitment is 12 months, after which you can continue month-to-month or cancel anytime. If you cancel, we simply remove the equipment. It's like a lawn service, but quieter, cheaper, and your lawn gets cut daily.

Is Mowing as a Service cheaper than a lawn crew?

In most cases, yes. Traditional lawn services in Westchester typically run $200-$400/month during the mowing season. Our MaaS plans start at $99/month and your lawn gets mowed daily instead of weekly — with zero emissions and no noisy crews.
