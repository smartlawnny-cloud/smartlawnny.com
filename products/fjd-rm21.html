Why RM21 for Your Property

The RM21 is not a residential mower scaled up. It is purpose-built for commercial and estate applications.

Widest Deck in Class

At 25 inches, the RM21 has the widest cutting deck of any autonomous mower. Fewer passes means faster coverage and less wear on your turf.

Multi-Functional Platform

Five interchangeable modules make this more than a mower. Ball picking, line marking, grass collecting, and dew brushing are all available on one machine.

Reduce Labor Costs

One RM21 can replace a full-time mowing crew. It operates autonomously 24/7, rain or shine, with no overtime, no breaks, and no callbacks.

Better for Your Turf

Frequent, light cuts promote healthier grass. The RM21 can mow daily at under 65 dB, returning fine clippings as natural fertilizer.

No Wire Installation

Virtual boundaries mean no trenching, no buried wire, and no signal issues. Change your mowing zones in the app anytime.
